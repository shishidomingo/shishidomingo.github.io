OSU Fisheries and Wildlife Scholarships

Every year, Multnomah Anglers and Hunters is proud to present six students pursuing degrees in Fisheries and Wildlife Sciences at Oregon State University with a scholarship to help fund their studies. These students are building careers in managing and conserving the fish, wildlife, and habitats that we work to protect, and we are honored to have been a part of this tradition for over 70 years. The scholarship program is funded by the generosity of our members and donors like you!

Our scholarships are dedicated to the memory of special MA&H members who worked to make this program such an amazing success.

Milt Guymon Endowment Award

Milt Guymon (died October 1, 1980) was a tireless crusader and chronicler for sportsmen in Oregon. In addition to serving with Multnomah Anglers & Hunters, he was the press relations and education officer for the Oregon State Fish & Game Commission where he published countless newspaper and magazine articles and bulletins and advocated for sportsmen's rights with the United States Senate.

The Milt Guymon Endowment Award is awarded annually to a full-time junior, senior, or post-baccalaureate student who displays evidence of leadership, volunteerism, and potential for a successful career.

2020 Recipient: Brent Cardenas

Bill Schaeffer Memorial Scholarship

Bill Schaeffer was a longtime member of the Multnomah Anglers & Hunters and a passionate conservationist. He served as editor of the the Multnomah Anglers & Hunters bulletin, and he testified on behalf of Oregon sportsmen to the United States Senate to secure greater access to public lands for outdoorsmen. He also served as public relations director for the Oregon Wildlife Federation.

The Bill Schaeffer Memorial Scholarship is awarded annually to a sophomore student based on exemplary academic performance and involvement in community activities.

2020 Recipient: Marie Heuberger

Chan Schenck Conservation Scholarship

Chan Schenck (died December 24, 1974) was a well-known sportsman and conservationist in Oregon. He was a member of Multnomah Anglers & Hunters for 41 years. He also helped to found the Oregon Wildlife Federation, the Columbia River Sportsmen's Council, and the Pacific Northwest Conservation Council.

The Chan Schenck Conservation Scholarship is awarded annually to a junior or senior student based on exemplary academic performance and involvement in community activities.

2020 Recipient: Morgan Dally

William (Bill) Bogh Memorial Scholarships

William Bogh (died March 1, 2018), of the US Army 77th Infantry and 5th Air Force, was an ardent fisherman and hunter. He was a was a longtime member of Multnomah Anglers & Hunters, as well as the Oregon Hunters Association, Tillamook Anglers, Northwest Steelheaders, Ducks Unlimited, and Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation.

2020 Recipients: Mahima White & Vanessa Ramirez

Larry Cassidy Memorial Scholarship

Larry Cassidy (died November 15, 2014) was a lifelong avid fisherman, hunter, and conservationist. He owned and operated Stan's Sport Shop for many years, and worked with ODFW's hatchery programs.

2020 Recipient: Maizy Kesterson
